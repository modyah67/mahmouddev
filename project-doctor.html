University Doctor Management System

A fully integrated academic platform designed to empower university professors with smart attendance tracking, grading automation, content management, and real-time student communication.

Project Overview

The Challenge

Manual grading systems and disconnected tools created inefficiencies, time loss, and limited transparency in academic operations.

The Solution

A centralized digital system integrating grading, attendance tracking, lecture materials, and notifications into one seamless interface.

Smart Attendance

Face recognition, GPS verification, and QR-based lecture validation.

Advanced Grade Management

Real-time grading dashboard with performance analytics.

Lecture & Task Upload

Organized course materials and structured assignment management.

Instant Notifications

Automated alerts for lectures, grades, and academic updates.

Technology Stack

Development Stack

Flutter • Firebase • Face Recognition APIs • GPS APIs • QR Code System • REST APIs
